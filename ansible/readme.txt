Here is a template for organizing your playbooks and the resources you may use with them.

Read the readme.txt in each directory to get an idea of how these may be used.

In this root directory, there are the following directories and files:
.an - temp directory
files - files for upload
inventory - host files for various machines ansible may connect to.
playbooks - where your playbooks live.
roles - where your role files are stored
ansible.cfg - config file read by ansible
readme.txt - this file your reading.

There are many different ways to organize your playbook and resources so keep yourself open
to how you may organize them for your particular needs.
